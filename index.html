Questions

What is ShipVault?

ShipVault is a directory of 30 small, real webapps — one shipped per day — each solving one specific job for solo operators and creators. Every app has a free tier and a paid Pro upgrade.

What's the difference between buying one app and the All-Access Pass?

Buying a single app's Pro upgrade unlocks Pro on that one app only. The All-Access Pass unlocks Pro on every app in the vault at once, including apps that haven't shipped yet.

Is this real, live payment processing?

Yes. Every purchase — single-app or All-Access — runs through live Stripe checkout. You'll get a normal Stripe receipt by email.

How do I get my All-Access license key after paying?

You're redirected back here automatically after checkout, and your key appears on screen within a few minutes. It's also saved to your browser automatically. If you ever lose it, use the "Already have a key?" box below with the email you paid with.

Will future apps be included in my All-Access Pass automatically?

Yes — the pass unlocks every app currently in the vault and every app added afterward, at no extra cost.

Do I need an account?

No. Apps run entirely in your browser. Pro unlocks are tied to your device via a license key, not a login.
